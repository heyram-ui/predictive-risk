Health Recommendations

Evidence-based daily lifestyle tips to reduce your disease risk

How our ML uses these recommendations

After predicting your risk profile, the system cross-references your specific risk factors against these guidelines to generate your personalized action plan in the PDF report.

Diabetes Prevention
Diet
  • Choose whole grains over white rice/bread
  • Limit sugar to <25g/day (WHO standard)
  • Eat high-fiber vegetables at every meal
  • Avoid sugary drinks (soda, juice)
  • Include bitter gourd, fenugreek in diet
Exercise
  • 150 min/week moderate aerobic exercise
  • Strength training 2–3x per week
  • Walk 10 minutes after each meal
  • Avoid sitting for more than 30 min straight
  • Aim for 7,000–10,000 steps daily
Lifestyle
  • Maintain BMI between 18.5–24.9
  • Sleep 7–8 hours per night
  • Monitor blood glucose quarterly
  • Reduce stress with yoga/meditation
  • Quit smoking — 2x diabetes risk factor
Heart Disease Prevention
Diet
  • DASH diet — reduce sodium to <2g/day
  • Eat oats, salmon, berries, and nuts
  • Avoid trans fats and processed meats
  • Include olive oil instead of butter
  • Limit alcohol — max 1 drink/day
Exercise
  • Brisk walking 30 min/day, 5 days a week
  • Swimming or cycling for cardio health
  • Avoid sudden vigorous exertion
  • Yoga reduces heart risk by 30–40%
  • Monitor pulse during exercise
Monitoring
  • Check BP every 6 months (target <120/80)
  • Annual cholesterol check after age 35
  • Keep resting heart rate 60–100 bpm
  • ECG check if you feel palpitations
  • Manage stress — cortisol damages arteries
Sleep & Mental Health
Better Sleep Habits
  • Sleep and wake at the same time daily
  • No screens 1 hour before bed
  • Keep room cool (18–20°C) and dark
  • Avoid caffeine after 2 PM
  • Try Yoga Nidra or Viparita Karani pose
  • 7–9 hours is the WHO recommended target
Mental Wellness
  • Practice deep breathing for 5 min daily
  • Limit news/social media to 30 min/day
  • Journal your thoughts each morning
  • Maintain social connections — talk daily
  • Seek professional help if stress score >7
  • Exercise releases serotonin — move daily!
