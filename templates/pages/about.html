About Health Risk

A Machine Learning-powered health risk prediction platform that analyzes your daily lifestyle data to predict potential health risks and provide personalized recommendations.

What This Project Does

Health Risk is a comprehensive health risk prediction web application that uses Machine Learning and rule-based analysis to:

  • Predict diseases — Analyzes 500+ potential health conditions based on your symptoms, vitals, and lifestyle data
  • Assess health risks — Uses a Random Forest ML model trained on 70,000+ medical records to calculate personalized risk scores
  • Check symptoms — Match your symptoms against 25+ disease profiles with 3-phase recovery plans
  • Find nearby care — Locate hospitals and pharmacies near you using Google Maps integration
  • Track recovery — Log daily health progress, diet quality, and symptom severity over time
  • Generate reports — Download branded PDF health reports to share with your doctor
  • Emergency alerts — Automatically notify emergency contacts when risk scores are critically high

How It Works

1
You Share Your Daily Routine

Enter your sleep hours, physical activity, stress level, BMI, blood pressure, screen time, and other lifestyle data into the assessment form.

2
Machine Learning Analyzes Your Data

Our RandomForest model (trained on real medical datasets) processes your input alongside 500+ rule-based disease conditions to calculate risk probabilities for each condition.

3
You Get Personalized Results

Receive a comprehensive health report with: overall risk score, top disease predictions with confidence levels, personalized lifestyle recommendations, and a downloadable PDF report.

4
Track & Improve Over Time

Use the daily tracker to log your recovery progress, diet quality, and symptom severity. View 7-day trend charts to see how you're improving.

Datasets & Training Data

This project uses 12+ real-world health datasets covering:

  • Diabetes risk factors and blood sugar patterns
  • Cardiovascular disease indicators
  • Sleep quality and insomnia metrics
  • Mental health scores (stress, anxiety, depression)
  • BMI and obesity pattern analysis
  • Blood pressure monitoring logs
  • Respiratory health markers
  • Nutrition and diet quality assessments
  • Physical activity and exercise tracking
  • Kidney function and liver health indicators
  • Bone density and musculoskeletal health

The ML model achieves ~98% accuracy on test data using a RandomForest classifier with stratified cross-validation.
